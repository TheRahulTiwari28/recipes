chilli paneer sizzler

Paneer Chilli Sizzlers Winter is upon us and its time to heat up a few things in kitchen to keep us warm. My personal favorite is the good old "Sizzler". Now widely available all across the world including most of the Indian restaurants. This is my take on the "Sizzler".

prep time

15 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Desert

instructions

step 1

For fried rice heat the pan add oil & cook the veggies for 5 min the. Add spices, sauces, then rice, mix it well & cook it well.

step 2

Now fry the lotus stem, finger chips, lady finger (first make by putting spices in between the lady finger& coated with Cornflour).

step 3

Fry the chaap as coated with Cornflour & spices & shallow fry paneer pieces also coated with Cornflour

step 4

Now make chilli paneer gravy by heating pan add oil, garlic, ginger, chillies once they cooked add onion & saut√© them

step 5

Now add sauces & spices, sugar & cook it well for 10 mins at last add water, when it come to boil add cornflour mix with water, cook it till it get thick.

step 6

Now heat the sizzler plate grease butter & start placing the items.

step 6

Before serving pour the chilli paneer gravy & serve hot.

ingredients

For Fried rice: 1 tbsp Oil, 1/2 Capsicum chopped, 1/4 cup Pea, 1/4 cup Sweet corn, 1/2 cup French beans, 1/2 Carrot chopped, 1 pinch Pepper, 2 tsp Sugar, 2 tsp Soya sauce, 2 cups Cooked rice

For Chilli paneer:, 400 gms Paneer, As required Cornflour for coating, As required Oil for fry, 1 tbsp Oil, 7-8 cloves Garlic, 2-3 Green chillies

1 cup skim milk

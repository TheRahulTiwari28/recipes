Eggless Omelette

Can you imagine enjoying an omelette without eggs? This might sound impossible but this delicious Eggless Bread Omelette recipe will leave you drooling over its amazing taste and texture. To make this quick and healthy Eggless Omelette, you just need a few simple ingredients in place and you can easily make this simple recipe. Just following some simple steps given below and share your feedback with us.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Desert

instructions

step 1

Prepare the batter : To begin with this easy recipe, trim the slices and add keep them aside. Make the batter with gram flour, refined flour, salt to taste, sugar, and saffron water. Next, add milk, water and baking soda, whisk it well. Make sure there's no lumps or you can blend it with the help of a hand blender. Next, take a non-stick pan, add butter once it's melted, pour it into the batter bowl and whisk it again nicely. Keep aside for further use.

step 2

Sauté the veggies : In the same non-stick pan, add oil/ butter. Once it's hot add some onion, green chilli, ginger, tomato and sauté it well. Add coriander leaves and sauté for a minute. Now, pour a ladle full of batter and rotate the pan so that it spreads evenly all around. Cook till the underside is done and the edges appear cooked. Next, add grated cheese on one side and roll.

step 3

Make the veggies : Take another pan and add oil, once it's hot, add tomato and potato. Season it with salt and pepper and cook until it's light brown in color. Serve hot with charred tomatoes and potatoes.

step 4

Prepare the Bread Omelette : To the same pan, add oil once it's hot, add remaining onion, green chilli, ginger and saute it well. Add tomatoes, coriander leaves and saute for a minute. Pour a ladle full of batter and rotate the pan so that it spreads evenly all around. Flip the omelette on the other side, add grated cheese and fold it from all the sides. Apply butter on both the bread slices and place the other bread slice on top and then flip it so that the other bread slice gets toasted. Once the surface crisps up, take it off heat. Serve hot.

ingredients

1/2 teaspoon refined oil

1 medium chopped onion

1 teaspoon butter

2 green chilli

1/2 inch ginger

1 tomato

1 tablespoon coriander leaves

1/4 cup processed cheese

1 slices bread slices

1 cup gram flour (besan)

1/3 cup refined flour

salt as required

1/4 teaspoon sugar

1 tablespoon soaked saffron

1 cup milk

3/4 cup water

1 teaspoon baking soda

2 tablespoon butter

black pepper as required

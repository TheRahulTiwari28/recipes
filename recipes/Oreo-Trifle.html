Oreo Trifle Recipe

Fond of chocolates? Then try this easy-to-make Chocolate Trifle that you can easily make at home with just 3 ingredients. Oreo Trifle is a creamy dessert that you can prepare for any occasion. If you are not in a mood to prepare a lavish dessert, then this Trifle recipe is perfect for you. You just need oreo biscuits, whipping cream and a chocolate bar to make this dessert. Oreo biscuits are loved by kids, so this dessert will be an instant hit among them. Be it a kids party or a family get together, you can always serve this dish as a dessert and impress everyone. Garnish the Trifle with some more crushed oreos, chocolate chips or any other topping of your choice to make the dessert more visually appealing. prep time

10 min.

cook time

2 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Desert

instructions

step 1

Crush the biscuits : Add the oreo biscuits to a ziplock bag and seal it. Now use a rolling pin to beat the biscuits. Crush the oreos properly to form small chunks.

step 2

Now take the chocolate bar and chop it properly. Add the crushed chocolate to a glass bowl and use a double boiler method to melt the chocolate properly.

step 3

Whisk the whipping cream : Now add whipping cream to a bowl and use an electric whisk to whisk it. Keep whisking till soft peaks are formed. Take half of the whipping cream and mix it with the melted chocolate.

step 4

Do the layering : Now take a dessert glass or bowl and add a layer of crushed oreos at the bottom. Next, add a layer of chocolate mixture and lastly add a layer of whipping cream.

step 5

Garnish and serve : Garnish with some more crushed oreos in the end. Your 3-ingredients Mousse Trifle is ready to be served.

ingredients

8 oreo cookies

50 gm milk chocolate

150 gm whipping cream

Vegetable Pulao

Those who love rice will certainly like to try this Vegetable Pulao recipe, which can be easily made at home. Here is the easiest way to make this rice recipe explained in detail with step-by-step images. We all love eating and there are times when we love to indulge in some really good food. What if you are fasting or just don't have access to anything non-veg and you still feel like gorging on some yummy delicacy? Vegetable Pulao to the rescue! It is one of the easiest rice recipes that make for a wholesome meal. It is quick and has many variations like peas pulao, mushroom pulao, paneer pulao etc. This variation of vegetable pulao has mixed vegetables and soya making it rich in protein.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

3 servings

Tags : Breakfast Lunch Dinner Desert

instructions

step 1

Saute onions until translucent and then fry vegetables in it : Add oil to a non-stick frying pan and heat over medium flame. Once the oil is sufficiently hot, add onions and stir till they become light pink. Make sure that you are stirring continuously else the onions might stick to the bottom of the pan. Once the onions turn slightly translucent, add in all the whole spices, along with the vegetables (not sprouts and soya). Cook for 2 minutes and then add chopped tomatoes in it.

step 2

Cook all the vegetables until tender : Stir the ingredients well and cook (covered with 1/4 cup water) until the hardest vegetable (in this case carrots) is slightly tender. This should take about 5-8 minutes. Make sure that the flame is on low-moderate flame.

step 3

Add sprouts & soya along with soaked brown rice : After 5-8 minutes, give it a check. If the vegetables have been cooked nicely, add sprouts and soya and stir for 2-3 minutes on moderate flame. Once this is done, add the drained brown rice, with salt and chilli powder. You can also add any other favourite masalas at this point. Stir till the rice starts forming clumps.

step 4

Let the rice cook with veggies until done and serve hot : Then, add 2 cups of water and bring the whole pot to a boil. After the first boil, simmer and cook covered, till the rice is cooked (usually around 8 minutes). Turn off the heat and transfer vegetable pulao in a serving bowl. Serve hot with boondi or cucumber raita.

ingredients

1 cup brown basmati rice

1 medium diced onion

1 inch cinnamon stick

1 teaspoon clove

1/2 teaspoon peppercorns

2 black cardamom

1 teaspoon red chilli powder

1 teaspoon salt

2 cup water

1 teaspoon virgin olive oil

4 tomato
